Agriexpress.ie is based in Ballinahown, Athlone, Co. Westmeath close to the Offaly border. The company was established in 1986 by the late Michael Rohan and his wife Annette, and has continued to be a family run business since. Set in the countryside amidst the farming community business has continued to be fruitful over the years.

We have always strived to meet our customer's requirements while offer a friendly service. We stock over 3000 products in store with a wide range also available on next day delivery. We continue to move with the times and seek out products that will benefit our customers currently and also in the future. We stock a wide product range including Animal dosing, Fertilisers, All animal feeds, Fencing, Fertilisers, Seeds, Troughs, Hardware, Building supplies and Pet supplies.

Our website has been designed for ease of use and navigation. Our checkout and payment system has also been designed for ease of use however if you prefer to order and pay over the phone, or have an enquiry about a product do not hesitate to call on (090) 6430244 and a member of our staff will be happy to help.

You can also email us on rohanagri@eircom.net
